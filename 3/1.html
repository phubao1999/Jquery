Blue Marine Foundation launches the fourth

London to Monaco cycle ride

17th – 24th September 2019

UK / Spain / France / Monaco

After another hugely successful year of the London to Monaco cycle ride which raised an impressive £456,000 for ocean conservation this year, we are excited to be launching the 4th edition which will take place between 17-24 September 2019.

For all inquiries about taking part or becoming a 2019 sponsor please email: info@london-monaco.cc
